Online Registration & Services Details

Registration procedure

Users can register through the website by selecting a specific course and clicking on the register button. The pricing for the course and the duration are mentioned upfront before the registration. Users can enquire by sending mail prior to registering. Users can click register which enables them to register for the selected course and do the online payment.

Courses offered

Certified UX-UI Designer(Working Professtionals) is conducted as weekly training program for 6 months (Saturday or Sunday from 10:00 am to 5:00 pm)

Certified UX-UI Designer(College Freshers) is conducted on weekdays for a span of 6 months (Monday - Friday from 10:00 am to 5:00 pm)

Certified UX Designer is conducted as weekly training program for 4 months (Saturday or Sunday from 10:00 am to 5:00 pm)

Certified UI Designer is conducted as weekly training program for 2.5 months (Saturday or Sunday from 10:00 am to 5:00 pm)

Accelerated UX program is conducted as 10 days continuous program to facilitate student from other cities and abroad.

Pricing structure for Courses

The pricing structure for the Certified UX-UI Designer(for Working Professtionals & College Freshers) course is Rs.60,000 + tax 10,800/- excluding any accommodation cost.

The pricing structure for the Certified UX Designer course is Rs.46,610 + tax 8,390/- excluding any accommodation cost. The pricing structure for the Certified UI Designer course is Rs.25,424/ + tax 4,576/- excluding any accommodation cost.

The pricing structure for the Accelerated UX program course is Rs.46,610 + tax 8,390/- excluding any accommodation cost.
